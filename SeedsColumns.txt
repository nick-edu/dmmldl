Dataset of three wheat varieties and their characteristics.
Source: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/seeds

Columns:
area (A)
perimeter (P)
compactness, C = 4*pi*A/P^2
length: length of the kernel
width: width of the kernel
asymmetry: asymmetry coefficient 
groove_length: length of kernel groove
variety: wheat variety: Kama, Rosa or Canadian, 70 elements each
